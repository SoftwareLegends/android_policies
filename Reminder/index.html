Privacy Policy for Reminder App

    Reminder is an app provided by Newroz Telecom to help users create and manage their special events. This Privacy Policy outlines how the app collects, uses, and shares information from its users.


  1. Information Collection

    The App collects the following types of information:

    • Account Information

      * When you create an account, we collect your email address and password.

    • Reminder Data

      * We collect information about your reminders, including dates, times, and content.

  2. Information Use To Provide and Maintain Our Service

    * We use your information to create and manage your account and to provide you with the features and functionality of the app.

    To Communicate with You

    * We may use your email address to send you updates, notifications, and other information related to your use of the app.

  3. Information Sharing

    * The information collected by the app is confidential and not shared with any third parties.

  4. App Usage

    * This application is intended to be used only by Newroz Telecom employees and cannot be used by any other users.

  5. App permissions

    * Account Information: We collect your email address to send a verification code for your email.

    6. * Reminder Data: We collect information about your reminders, including dates, times, and content, to send you notifications based on your selected dates and times.
