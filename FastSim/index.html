FastSIM Privacy Policy

FastSIM is an app provided by the Fastlink Telecom company to digitalize the sale process of their SIM cards, based on the request of the government authorities, to the subscribers through multiple Points-of-Sale (POS) and hundreds of local agents in the market across the country.


This Privacy Policy describes how the App collects, uses, and shares information from users.

  1. Information Collected

    The App collects the following types of information:

    • Location data

      The app uses location to determine the correct location latitude and longitude of the agents and POS as requested by the government authorities in the Kurdistan Region of Iraq.

    • Camera

      The app uses the camera to scan the documents of the subscribers and take their pictures who purchas SIM cards, the app opens the camera only when the POS agent starts the sale process by pressing the button [Take Picture(s)]. These pictures are then sent to the BackOffice to validate their correctness and based on these pictures the SIM purchase is either accepted or rejected.

    • Files

      The app uses Files permission to allow the POS agents to change their profile pictures whenever they require.

  2. Information Use

    The information collected by the app is sent to the companies (Fastlink Telecom and Allai Newroz Telecom) and is stored in a highly secured infrastructure.

  3. Information Sharing

    The information collected by the App is confidential and not shared with any third parties.

  4. App Usage

    This application is intended for the use of the POS and shops that sell Fastlink and Newroz SIMs only and cannot be used by any kind of user.

  5. App permissions

    The app uses following permissions:

    • Location – to have access to the device location and to be able to store the location of the POS and Shop and show them on a map to allow people to find the points of sale that sell their SIMs.
    • Camera – to scan the documents of the subscribers and take their pictures during the sale. Also, to allow the agents to take their own pictures for their profile picture.
    • Files – to allow the POS agents and shop owners to change their profile pictures.
